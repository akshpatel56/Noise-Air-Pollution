About Pollution Monitor

Transparent, real-time pollution data for a healthier India

Our Mission

To provide accurate, real-time air and noise pollution data to empower citizens, inform policymakers, and create cleaner, healthier cities across India.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Pollution Monitor began as a small project to visualize government pollution data in a more accessible format. Today, we aggregate data from multiple sources including CPCB, SAFAR, and hundreds of monitoring stations across India.

Our platform serves over 1 million monthly users including researchers, students, environmental activists, and concerned citizens.

Our Data Sources

We collect and process data from:

  • Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB)
  • SAFAR Air Quality System
  • State Pollution Control Boards
  • NASA Satellite Data
  • Ground-based Monitoring Stations
