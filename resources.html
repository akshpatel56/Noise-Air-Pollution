Pollution Health & Safety Resources

Protect yourself and your family from air and noise pollution

Immediate Protection Tips

Wear N95/N99 Masks

Use certified masks when AQI is above 150. Change masks every 8 hours of use.

Create Clean Air Spaces

Use air purifiers with HEPA filters. Keep windows closed during high pollution hours.

Air Purifying Plants

Keep snake plants, peace lilies, and spider plants indoors to improve air quality.

Noise Protection

Use earplugs or noise-cancelling headphones in areas with noise above 75 dB.
