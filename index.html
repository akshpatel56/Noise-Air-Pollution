Health Advisory

Good (0-50 AQI)

Air quality is satisfactory. No health impacts expected.

Moderate (51-100)

Acceptable air quality. Sensitive individuals should limit prolonged outdoor exertion.

Unhealthy (101-150)

General public may experience health effects. Sensitive groups should avoid outdoor activity.

Health warning of emergency conditions. Everyone should avoid all outdoor activity.
